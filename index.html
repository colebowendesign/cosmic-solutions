03 The Constellation

A small studio.
An infinite field.

Cosmic Solutions is a strategy studio founded on the belief that the most important problems resist easy framing. We work with institutions, founders, and cultural organisations navigating the space between where they are and where they need to be.

  • 01

    Precision over volume

    We take fewer engagements, and go deeper. The brief is always small enough to act on and large enough to matter.

  • 02

    The long view

    We design for decades, not quarters. Our work functions as a compass long after we have left the room.

  • 03

    Craft as rigour

    Every document, deck, and artefact we produce is made with the same care as the strategy it contains.
